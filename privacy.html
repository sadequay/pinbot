Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

This privacy policy covers the personal automation tool operated under this site (referred to below as "the app"). The app is used solely by its developer for personal purposes and is not distributed to or used by the public.

What this app does

The app connects to the developer's own Pinterest account to automatically create and publish pins to boards owned by that same account. It does not interact with, collect data from, or act on behalf of any other Pinterest user.

Data collection

The app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data belonging to any third party. The only data it processes is:

  • The developer's own Pinterest authentication tokens, used solely to authorize posting to the developer's own account
  • Content the developer supplies themselves (product images, links, and text) for the purpose of creating pins

Data storage

Authentication credentials are stored as encrypted secrets within the app's private hosting environment and are never exposed publicly or shared with any third party.

Third-party services

The app communicates only with the official Pinterest API to publish content. No other third-party services receive data from this app.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes to the app. The effective date above will be updated accordingly.
